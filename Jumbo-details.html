Jumbo Adventure

Jumbo Adventure is an exciting 3D game developed using Unity. In this project, I worked as a Story Developer and Animator, contributing to two key stories within the game. My responsibilities included:

  • Developing engaging storylines that enhance the player's experience
  • Creating and managing animations for characters and environments
  • Implementing and fine-tuning the Timeline feature for seamless story progression
  • Collaborating with team members to ensure cohesive integration of story elements with gameplay

This project showcases my skills in narrative design, animation, and Unity development, demonstrating my ability to contribute to multiple aspects of game creation.
